Read this before drawing any conclusions

This backtest shows hypothetical results of applying a rules-based strategy to historical data. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

  • Gold dependency. Gold contributed roughly 30% of total returns — a reliance on gold's 1996-2026 outperformance that may not repeat.
  • Monthly data. Drawdowns shown are month-end to month-end. Intramonth lows during crises likely were 2-5pp worse.
  • Bull market lag. The strategy is designed to underperform buy-and-hold during sustained bull markets. That's structural, not a flaw.
  • Behavioral discipline. The alpha is concentrated in crisis years. An investor who abandons the rules during underperformance captures the worst of both worlds.
  • Framework limits. 2022's rate-shock drawdown was only partially captured. Pure-rate-driven declines are outside the framework's core design.
