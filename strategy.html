Strategy decision logic (in priority order):
1. L4 Confirmation Override (100% stock / 0% bond / 0% gold) — when L4 peaked ≥70 in the last 7 months and has dropped ≥20pts.
2. L5 Defensive Override (0% stock / 30% gold / 70% bond) — when both 6-month trailing returns of S&P 500 AND gold are negative.
3. Base stage from max(L1,L2): Stage 1 (Calm) 95/5/0 when <35 · Stage 2 (Moderate) 55/35/10 when 35–<80 · Stage 3 (Stress) 0/30/70 when ≥80.

"Bond" sleeve composition: 50% SHY (1–3yr U.S. Treasuries) + 50% IEF (7–10yr U.S. Treasuries). Bond returns reflect 30% U.S. withholding tax on coupon distributions and 15bps ETF expense ratio.

Pricing & execution: All prices, returns, and rebalances use month-end closing prices. Trades are executed at the close of the last trading day of the month. The 2-month signal lag means a rebalance at month-end M uses Layer scores dated month-end M−2 (observable around day 20 of month M−1).
